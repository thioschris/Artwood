Our Story

27 years of artistry, pride, and passion in every creation

Artistry in Craft

The beauty and visual impact of an Artwood creation is what makes it truly distinctive. More than fine materials and exceptional quality, Artwood craftsmen bring true artistry, pride, and passion to every project.

For over 27 years, Artwood has transformed beautiful timbers into architectural masterpieces of elegance and style. Our work often incorporates other natural materials such as glass, copper, stone, and steel, carefully blended to complement the concept, function, and character of each space—whether modern or traditional, formal or casual.

Our Scope of Work

Artwood undertakes commissions for private clients, designers, and architects, and collaborates closely with construction and contracting companies. Our scope of work is extensive and diverse, ranging from bespoke furniture to large-scale architectural structures.

We are deeply involved in projects including building renovations (hotels, commercial spaces, offices, and private residences), new-build constructions, and specialised timber applications such as roofs, pergolas, and wall cladding. Custom-made furniture and unique architectural elements are central to our portfolio.

Founded in Craftsmanship

Established in 1997 by Savvas Savva and Giannakis Alexandrou, Artwood relocated in 2005 to its purpose-built facilities in Ayios Sylas, Limassol. Since then, we have continued to uphold the tradition of true craftsmanship.

Our philosophy has remained unchanged: to deliver beautifully crafted work, at competitive prices, and always on time. Artwood is a true master of its craft—explore our gallery to be inspired.
